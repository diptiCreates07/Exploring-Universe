VENUS

☁️ Earth's Twin Planet

Quick Facts

  • 🪐 Venus is the 2nd planet from the Sun
  • 🌡️ It is the hottest planet in our solar system
  • ☁️ Thick clouds of sulfuric acid cover it
  • 🔄 It rotates in the opposite direction (retrograde rotation)
  • 🌍 It is often called Earth’s “sister planet” because of similar size
