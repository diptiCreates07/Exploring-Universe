SATURN

🪐 The Jewel of the Solar System

Quick Facts

  • 🪐 Saturn is the 6th planet from the Sun
  • 💍 It is famous for its beautiful ring system
  • 🌪️ It is a gas giant made mostly of hydrogen and helium
  • 🌕 It has 80+ moons
  • 🌬️ It has very fast winds in its atmosphere
