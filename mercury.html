MERCURY

🔥 The Closest Planet to the Sun

Quick Facts

  • ☿️ Mercury is the closest planet to the Sun
  • 🌡️ It has extreme temperature changes
  • 🌑 It has no atmosphere
  • 🪨 It is a rocky and cratered planet
  • ⏱️ A day is longer than its year in some cases
