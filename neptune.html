Quick Facts

  • 🌊 Neptune is the 8th planet from the Sun
  • 💨 It has the fastest winds in the solar system
  • 🧊 It is an ice giant made of gas and ices
  • 🌙 It has 14 known moons
  • 🌌 It takes 165 Earth years to orbit the Sun
